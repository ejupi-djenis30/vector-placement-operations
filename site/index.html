03 Product principles

Sensitive work needs
boring guarantees.

01

Use less data.

Collect only what the placement process requires. Contact data does not belong in a public demonstration.

02

Show the state.

Progress, next actions and ownership stay visible. A status should never be a mystery buried in a document.

03

Keep an exit.

Every local change can be reset. Production systems should add export, retention and deletion controls from day one.

04 Team provenance

Rebuilt for the public. True to its team roots.

VECTOR began as a 2023 academic team project. This edition keeps the product idea and rebuilds the application with synthetic fixtures and a browser-only data model.

The contributors approved publication of this reconstruction. The code is available under the MIT License.
